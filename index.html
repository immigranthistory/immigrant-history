Telling Untold Stories.


The Immigrant History Initiative seeks to educate and empower communities through the untold stories of immigrant diasporas in America.

The Immigrant History Initiative is a non-profit organization founded in 2018. The organization produces curriculum focused on immigrant histories and works with schools and communities to establish courses sharing this knowledge. We also provide teaching and course development support in partnership with other organizations bringing immigrant histories into the mainstream.
